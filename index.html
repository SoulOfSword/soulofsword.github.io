About me

I was born in Italy near Milan, and at 13 years old I moved to Stockholm, Sweden, where attended the International School of the Stocholm Region, pursuing my IB diploma which I obtained in 2021. I decided to continue my academic career in the Netherlands, where I have since then studied Astronomy and obtained my Bachelor's Degree in 2024 (cum laude). I have also joined the Honors College of the University during this time. I am now pursuing my Master's.
